Dimension 5 Golf

Automated The Collection of Golf Data to Improve Peoples Score

Dimension 5 Golf is about improving people's golf game. And one of the problems is knowing people's actual performance as people aren't recording their data other than a score on the golf course. Secondly, its bright outside and so electronic devices are hard to use. So, we took a two prongued approach to this.

First, we used a printable version of the golf course to record your data on, which included what club you used and where you hit it. Then, you could put it online later using a google maps version of the system to indicate exactly where you hit it and what club you used. That solved the problem to be able to collect the data in a way that couldn't easily be done before.

Susquehanna International Group

Automatically Did PnL and Analyzed Outstanding Risk of Entire Firm
